# ⚖️ YouTube Rules — Channel Safe Rakhne Ke Liye

**Maqsad:** Channel kabhi strike ya band na ho. Har short banane se pehle ye checklist dekho.
Ye rules YouTube ki official policies pe based hain (YPP, Community Guidelines, Copyright).

---

## ✅ HAR SHORT BANANE SE PEHLE — Checklist

- [ ] Content **100% original AI** hai? (script + image + voice apna banaya, kisi ka copy nahi)
- [ ] Koi doosre ka video/clip/cartoon **use nahi** kiya?
- [ ] Music **YouTube Audio Library** ya royalty-free hai? (random song mat lagao)
- [ ] Title/thumbnail **clickbait-jhooth nahi** — jo dikhaya wahi kahani ho?
- [ ] [USED-IDEAS.md](USED-IDEAS.md) me check kiya — ye kahani **duplicate to nahi**?
- [ ] Content me **gaali, hinsa, ya darr-fear inatna nahi** ki age-restrict ho jaaye?

---

## 🟢 KYA ALLOWED HAI (safe)

| ✅ Allowed | Kyun safe |
|---|---|
| Apni likhi kahani | Original — tera copyright |
| AI-generated images (Bing/Leonardo) | Tune banayi, kisi ki nahi |
| AI voice (ElevenLabs, etc.) | Text-to-speech, original |
| YouTube Audio Library music | YouTube khud free deta hai |
| Royalty-free / CC0 assets | License allow karta hai |

## 🔴 KYA MANA HAI (strike/ban ka khatra)

| ❌ Mana | Kya hoga |
|---|---|
| Movie/cartoon/web-series clip | Content ID → strike / demonetize |
| Doosre ka video "drawing/filter" laga ke | Derivative work → phir bhi copyright |
| Kisi aur ka gaana/song | Copyright claim |
| Copy-paste script (kisi aur ke channel se) | Reused content → reject |
| Same video baar-baar upload | Duplicate → spam flag |
| Clickbait jhooth (title me kuch, video me kuch) | Community strike |
| Fake "sacchi ghatna" jo sensitive ho | Misinformation flag |

---

## 💰 Monetization (YPP) ke liye zaroori

YouTube Partner Program join karne ke liye chahiye:
- **1,000 subscribers** AUR
- **10 million** valid Shorts views (90 din me) — YA — 4,000 watch hours (long videos)
- Plus: content **original + advertiser-friendly** ho

**Sabse zaroori:** YouTube "original + transformative" content hi monetize karta hai.
Humara content 100% AI-original hai → ye condition **poori** hoti hai ✅

---

## 🛡️ 3-Strike Rule (yaad rakho)

- **1st strike:** warning (1 week upload band)
- **2nd strike:** 2 week upload band
- **3rd strike (90 din me):** ❌ **Channel permanently delete**

Isliye — **kabhi bhi doosre ka content copy mat karo.** Ek galti = mehnat barbaad.

---

## 📌 Golden Rule

> "Agar ye content maine khud AI se nahi banaya, to upload mat karo."
> Bas isi ek line ko follow karo — channel hamesha safe rahega. ✅
