## 📝 VOICE SCRIPT (dheemi, emotional + suspense)

**[STORY — 3-33s]**
> "Main uth ke baitha. Phone uthaya. Behen ka message — 'Bhaiya, jaldi. Darwaza mat kholna. Kisi ko andar mat aane dena.'
> Maine call kiya — phone band. Woh to nani ke ghar gayi thi, doosre sheher.
> Tabhi… darwaze pe dastak. Dheere se. Ek… do… teen.
> Maine peephole se dekha — koi nahi tha.
> Phir ek aur message aaya — 'Bhaiya… woh ab andar aa gaya hai. Peeche mat dekhna.'"

**[TWIST — 33-42s]**
> "Meri saans ruk gayi. Ghar me to sirf main tha…
> Agli subah pata chala — meri behen ka phone teen din pehle kho gaya tha.
> Aur us raat… koi mere peeche khada tha."

**[CTA — 42-45s]**
> "Aisi aur kahaniyan sunne ke liye — follow karo."
