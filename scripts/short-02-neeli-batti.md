## 📝 VOICE SCRIPT (dheemi, darawni awaaz)

**[HOOK — 0-3s]**
> "Highway pe har raat ek neeli batti jalti thi. Log kehte the — us taraf mat jaana."

**[STORY — 3-33s]**
> "Us raat meri gaadi kharab ho gayi. Door tak koi nahi tha — sirf woh neeli batti.
> Maine socha shayad koi dhaba hoga. Main chal pada us taraf.
> Jitna chalta, batti utni door hoti jaati. Do ghante chalta raha… batti wahin ki wahin.
> Achanak ek awaaz — 'Ruk jao. Aage mat aana.'
> Maine palat ke dekha — ek boodha aadmi, haath me neeli lantern.
> Woh bola — 'Main 20 saal se logon ko rok raha hoon. Aage khaai hai. Us raat main nahi ruka tha.'"

**[TWIST — 33-42s]**
> "Main ghar pahuncha. Agle din news me dekha — us highway pe 20 saal pehle ek aadmi khaai me gir ke mar gaya tha.
> Us din se… woh har kisi ko rokta hai. Taaki koi aur uski tarah na mare."

**[CTA — 42-45s]**
> "Aisi aur kahaniyan sunne ke liye — follow karo."
