## 📝 VOICE SCRIPT (dheemi, darawni awaaz)

**[HOOK — 0-3s]**
> "Raat ke 12 baje… us bus stop pe sirf main tha. Aur woh aurat."

**[STORY — 3-33s]**
> "Bus aayi. Main chadha. Woh aurat sabse peeche wali seat pe baithi thi — muh dupatte se dhaka hua.
> Conductor aaya, sabse ticket li… par us aurat ke paas ja ke ruk gaya. Kuch nahi bola, aage badh gaya.
> Maine poocha — 'Bhaiya, unki ticket?'
> Conductor ne dheere se kaha — 'Sahab… us seat pe koi nahi baitha.'
> Mera dil ruk gaya. Maine peeche mud ke dekha…
> Seat khaali thi. Par uska dupatta… ab meri godi me pada tha."

**[TWIST — 33-42s]**
> "Aur us dupatte pe likha tha — ek naam. Mera naam.
> Us raat ke baad, main aaj tak us bus ka intezaar kar raha hoon.
> Kyunki log kehte hain — main bhi us raat ghar nahi pahuncha tha."

**[CTA — 42-45s]**
> "Aisi aur kahaniyan sunne ke liye — follow karo."
