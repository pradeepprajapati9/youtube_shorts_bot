## ✅ Rules — duplicate se bachne ke liye

1. **Same topic 2 baar mat use karo** (neeche list me check karo)
2. **Hook line kabhi repeat mat karo** — pehli 3 sec sabse important
3. **Twist alag rakho** — same "peeche koi khada tha" baar-baar mat
4. **Setting badalte raho** — bus, highway, ghar, jungle, hospital, school, gaon…
5. Bot banega to ye check **automatic** hoga (naye script ko purane se compare karega)
