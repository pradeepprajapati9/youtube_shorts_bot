# 🎯 STRATEGY — YouTube Algorithm ko apne favour me karo

**Tera sawaal (bilkul sahi):** Jab koi user ek type ka video 2-3 baar dekhta hai, to uska interest badhta hai aur waise hi videos dikhne lagte hain. Humara video bhi un logon tak pahunche jo pehle se aisi kahaniyan dekh rahe hain → views ban jaayein.

**Jawaab:** Haan, ye bilkul possible hai — YouTube ka algorithm **isi tarah kaam karta hai.** Neeche exact strategy hai.

---

## 🧠 Algorithm kaise kaam karta hai (simple)

```
1. Tu video daalta hai
2. YouTube pehle 200-500 logon ko dikhata hai (test)
3. Dekhta hai — kitne % log pura dekhte hain? (RETENTION)
   + like / comment / share / re-watch karte hain?
4. Agar log pasand karein → YouTube 5,000 ko dikhata
5. Phir 50,000… phir viral 🚀
6. YouTube khud dhoondta hai wo log jo "aisi hi kahaniyan" dekhte hain
   aur unke feed me tera video daal deta hai
```

**Matlab:** Tujhe audience khud nahi dhoondni. Agar **retention + engagement** achhi hai, to YouTube khud tera video **sahi logon tak** le jaata hai. Ye tera game hai.

---

## 🔑 5 cheezein jo "similar audience" tak pahunchati hain

### 1. **NICHE fix rakho** (sabse important)
Ek hi type ki kahani daalo — horror/suspense. Agar aaj horror, kal comedy, parso cooking daaloge to YouTube confuse ho jaata "iska video kise dikhaun?" → kisi ko nahi dikhata.
> **Rule:** 3-4 mahine sirf horror/suspense. YouTube samajh jaayega "ye horror channel hai" aur horror dekhne walon ko dikhayega.

### 2. **RETENTION** (log pura dekhein)
- Hook pehle **3 second** me (warna scroll)
- 30-45 sec chhota video (pura dekhne ke chance zyada)
- Twist last me (log ruke rahein)
- **Loop banao:** aakhri line pehli line se jode → log dobara dekhein (re-watch = algorithm boost)

### 3. **Same tags + hashtags** (YouTube ko batao ye kis type ka hai)
Har video pe same core tags: `hindi kahani, horror story, suspense, bhutiya kahani`
Isse YouTube same category ke doosre videos ke saath tera video group karta hai → wahi audience.

### 4. **SERIES banao** (binge effect)
Jaise "Raat ki Kahaniyan - Part 1, 2, 3…". Ek dekha to log agla dhoondhte hain.
> Playlist banao → ek video ke baad doosra auto-chale → watch time badhe.

### 5. **ENGAGEMENT trigger karo**
Video ke end me sawaal poocho:
- "Aapke saath aisa hua hai? Comment karo 👇"
- "Aage kya hua — bataao?"
Comment/like/share = YouTube ko signal "ye video accha hai" → zyada push.

---

## 📈 "Views compound" kaise hote (tera point)

Tu bilkul sahi samjha:
```
Ek video chal gaya
   → us video ke viewers ko tere PURANE videos bhi dikhne lage
   → wo bhi dekhe → un pe bhi views
   → channel ko "boost" mila
   → agla video aur zyada logon tak
```
Isiliye **consistency** sabse zaroori — jitne zyada videos, utne zyada "entry points", utna compounding.

| Kaam | Rule |

| Upload frequency | Roz 1 (ya kam se kam 4-5/week) |

| Hook | Pehle 3 sec me sabse strong line |
| End | Loop + comment ka sawaal |
| Hashtags | 3-5 same core (#hindikahani #horror #shorts) |

---

## 🚫 Kya MAT karo (algorithm todta hai)

- ❌ Niche baar-baar badalna (YouTube confuse)
- ❌ Lambe boring intro (pehle 3 sec me log bhaag jaate)
- ❌ Fake clickbait (log turant band karein → retention girti)
- ❌ Beech me upload band karna (algorithm channel ko "dead" maan leta)
- ❌ Views kharidna (permanent shadow-ban)

---

## 🎯 Bottom Line

> Audience dhoondhne ki zaroorat nahi — **achhi retention + consistency + fix niche** do,
> YouTube **khud** tera video un logon tak pahunchayega jo aisi kahaniyan dekhte hain.
> Bas pehle 10-15 videos me experiment karke dekho kaunsa hook/style chalta hai, phir usi pe double karo.
